WELCOME TO BARNSLEY.IO

BARNSLEY.IO is home to a passionate bunch of makers, hackers and doers based in Barnsley, South Yorkshire. If you live in or around Barnsley, and are interested in all things making, be it Arduinos and Raspberry Pis, 3D printers and lazer cutters or model making and crafts, then why not join us and get involved.

Our ultimate dream is to setup a dedicated makerspace in Barnsley, but right now we hold regular hack/social nights in various locations. To join our next meetup, head on over to Meetup.com to find out when and where and continue the discussion afterwards in our mailing list on Google Groups.

If you like your social media platforms then why not follow us on Twitter and Facebook or if all else fails just drop us an email at hi@barnsley.io
